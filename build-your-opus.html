Build Your OPUS

You must have Javascript enabled to use the Build Your OPUS tool.

Use the OPUS System Builder to customize OPUS scanning systems to suit your library's needs. You can configure up to five different systems and get an estimated price, including any quantity or institutional discounts. Please call us at 800-378-5432 to request your institution's discount code , and ask about any additional discounts that may apply.
