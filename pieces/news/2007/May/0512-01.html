New Hampshire Chooses Image Access/DLSG

BOCA RATON, FL.

May 12th, 2007

New Hampshire chooses Image Access and the DLSG to meet its university library digitization needs, purchasing a Knowledge Imaging Center (KIC II) for four universities and a Bookeye 3 for three universities. Beginning this fall, students and faculty will be able to scan books and other objects up to 17x24 inches in full color in five to fifteen seconds per scan. The three Bookeye 3s were purchased with glass plates and foot pedals and will be used to meet various digitization needs.
