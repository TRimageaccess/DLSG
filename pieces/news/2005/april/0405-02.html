Digital Library Systems Group is announcing a new, ultra-fast 600dpi flatbed with Opus Starter Kit

BOCA RATON, FL.

BOCA RATON, FL. APRIL, 2005

The Digital Library Systems Group at Image Access, a leader in innovative products for university libraries, is announcing a new, ultra-fast 600dpi flatbed with OPUS Starter Kit. Capture images and metadata, and produce myriad derivatives faster and better than systems costing four times as much. Opus has something for everyone. For the preservation expert, Opus can be expanded and customized, and for those that are just setting up digital preservation systems, Opus has a starter kit that lets you hit the ground running.
