The Digital Library Systems Group introduces a new package that incorporates our Bookeye color scanner and our WiFi scanning carts.

BOCA RATON, FL.

BOCA RATON, FL. APRIL, 2005

The Digital Library Systems Group at Image Access, a leader in innovative products for university libraries, is announcing a new package that incorporates our Bookeye Color scanner and our WiFi scanning carts. Both products greatly improve image quality and enhance existing products like ILLiad and Ariel by eliminating the keying. This new BSCAN ILL package now includes the Central Station for our WiFi scanning cart when purchased with a Bookeye color scanner.
