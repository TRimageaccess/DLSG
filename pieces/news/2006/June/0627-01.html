Two trips to Montana for one install.

BOCA RATON, FL.

June 27th, 2006

Was it really that a part of the Bookeye 3 shipment was lost in the Cascades by a local trucking company, or was it a convenient story so the DLSG field engineer (and avid fisherman) could make a second trip to Montana in June? Unversity of Montana's Bookeye 3 A1 was flown straight from the Image Access factory in Europe to Seattle and trucked to Montana. Somehow, a box was separated from the main crate and lost with no trace. Then a week later, it resurfaced and was finally delivered. Just another fish story?
