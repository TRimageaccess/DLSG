Iowa adds their first KIC II after having a KIC I for two years.

BOCA RATON, FL.

March 14th, 2007

The KIC I was voted for by the students to be purchased from their fund. It's a beautiful combination of hardwood and technology. However, the unit was a bit pricey. When KIC II came out at essentially half the price, we knew that it was only a matter of time before we got one. KIC II is actually attractive in a functional technology way. And it' s footprint is smaller. We' re excited to see how students react to the new unit.
