KIC is preferred by students 3 to one.

BOCA RATON, FL.

September 8th, 2006

Looking at 2006 usage statistics, Debora Morley, the Director of Information Technology Services in the Harvard College Library reported to us recently that while KIC represents 10% of their publicly accessible walk-up scanning systems, it is being used 25% of the time. This high usage difference probably couldn't happen unless many of the KIC users were walking past one or more of the other walk-up scanning systems. This also begs the question how much more scanning would be done if the library were full of KIC systems?
