First user at Notre Dame gets a kick out of KIC.

BOCA RATON, FL.

August 22nd, 2006

Diane Kennedy, a Notre Dame computer applications specialist at the Hesburgh Library, looks on as the first student walks up, scans, saves to a USB flash memory device, turns to her and says, "Wow, this is great." Since then, Diane reports that students frequently rave about KIC and evangelize KIC as a great new library resource to their friends.
