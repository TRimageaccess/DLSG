KIC Makes Animation Shorts

Providence, Rhode Island

June 15, 2009

Design school students find KIC perfect for creating animation shorts. Give them the tools and you can expect university students to find a way to use them in a manner that was not anticipated. In this animation short, more than 1,000 images were captured sequentially on the Bookeye to make a fascinating and well thought out movie.

Quoting the author, "All the pictures, I took in the library with 'Bookeye' which is an amazing scanner." See the movie on You Tube at http://www.youtube.com/user/jecy74
