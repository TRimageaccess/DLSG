More Side-by-Side KICs

Lawrence, Kansas

May 4, 2009

At the University of Kansas, having had prior experience with KIC systems located in their Arts & Architecture, Rare Books, Humanities & Social Sciences and Sciences libraries, an Associate Librarian told us they "had to purchase two KIC systems and put them side-by-side in their main library because the students are so elated with KIC that we have to purchase two to keep the lines down."
