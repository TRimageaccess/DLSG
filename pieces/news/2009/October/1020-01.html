Digitization Projects in the ‘Outback’ of Nevada

Nevada

October 20, 2009

Archivists and records managers are not likely to allow historic land records to be taken far from their sights. Such is the case with records that date back to the early frontier days in the Outback of Nevada. The scanner and technician traveled to these remote areas to perform the scanning on-site. The Bookeye 3 A1 was chosen because of its large size, glass plate, speed and resilience.
